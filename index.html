Tim Hortons

"Always Fresh. Always Tim Hortons."

It all started with a donut and a dream. With over 50 years of successful business, Tim Hortons has been a true part of a Canadian's soul from the beginning. Founded in the year 1964 in the beautiful city of Hamilton by hockey player Miles Gilbert "Tim" Horton, the franchise has come a long way since the day it opened. With hundreds of restaurants world-wide, the dream still lives on to this very day. Check out the website to learn more about Tim Hortons!
